Detailed Description

A encoded DataSetMessage.

This is usually retrieved from the IDataSetMessageEncoder and passed as part of the NetworkMessage to the INetworkMessageEncoder. Or in the other direction. It is retrieved from the INetworkMessageDecoder as part of the NetworkMessage and than passed to the IDataSetMessageDecoder.
